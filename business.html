Use fintech apps like Cred, PhonePe to pay credit card bills? July 1 could bring bad news

The Reserve Bank of India earlier directed that all credit card payments must be processed through the Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS) after June 30. HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank and Axis Bank have not activated BBPS. These banks have issued a combined over 5 crore credit cards to customers. Currently, only eight banks have activated bill payments on BBPS, as against a total of 34 banks that are allowed to issue credit cards. The lenders who have activated BBPS include SBI Card, BoB Card, IndusInd Bank, Federal Bank, and Kotak Mahindra Bank.
